F.L.A.R.E

F.L.A.R.E is an early warning fire system that analyzes fire risk using environmental data (temperature, humidity, air quality, wind speed, and rain levels). It incorporates integrated cellular communication for remote alerts and uses solar power and batteries for self-sufficiency.

This project focused not only on creating something to benefit the community but also allowed for expanding knowledge in engineering areas. This project taught and gave real-world exposure to 3D modeling, PCB design, and electronic integration, the scientific process including lots of trial and error, and different lenses to develop solutions for communities on small and large scales.
